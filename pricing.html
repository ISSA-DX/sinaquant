System FAQs

How often are technical reports published?

Professional members receive two major technical deep-dives per week, plus daily sector briefs and model-performance evaluations. The Sinaquant Data Terminal refreshes the underlying benchmark datasets every 24 hours.

Can I cancel my Professional subscription?

Yes. You can cancel at any time from your dashboard. Access remains active until the end of your current billing period, and we pro-rate refunds on annual plans.

What is included in the Enterprise API?

The Enterprise API provides RESTful endpoints for our proprietary sentiment engine, hardware benchmark database, and the Sinaquant Alpha signal stream. Rate limits and SLAs are tailored per contract.

Do you offer educational or non-profit pricing?

Yes — accredited universities and registered non-profits get 50% off the Professional tier. Contact us with proof of status and we'll set you up.
